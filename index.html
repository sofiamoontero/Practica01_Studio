Welcome to NYC's KASSETTE! We're here to rekindle the forgotten joy of music through cassette tapes, offering a diverse genre collection. Join us for music events and workshops that celebrate the essence of vintage tunes. Embrace the nostalgia, rediscover the classics!


Immerse yourself in unique experiences through our workshops, conferences, and lectures, all centered around the fascinating universe of cassettes.

Dive into the nostalgia of the golden years of music with our "RetroSound" workshop. We'll explore the magic of cassettes, from creating personalized mixes to restoring old tapes. Discover how analog technology remains relevant in the digital world.

Unleash your creativity in our interactive workshop! "Create Your Own Mixtape" will guide you through the process of selecting songs, designing the cover, and recording your mixtape on tape. Take home your unique creation and relive the vintage experience of sharing music.

Explore the fascinating intersection of analog and digital in our lecture "Cassettes 2.0". Discover how contemporary artists are embracing this classic format, merging it with current technology to create unique auditory experiences. Immerse yourself in the analog renaissance that is reshaping how we experience music.
